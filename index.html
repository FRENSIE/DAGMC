What is DAGMC?¶

The Direct Accelerated Geometry Monte Carlo [DAGMC] toolkit is a component of the Mesh-Oriented datABase [MOAB] that provides fundamental functions for the ray-tracing and related geometry operations of Monte Carlo radiation transport directly on complex 3-D geometries such as those created in modern solid modeling software.

DAGMC is designed in a modular fashion with the expectation that it can be integrated into a variety of Monte Carlo radiation tools. The CNERG group at UW-Madison has been focusing its development on the MCNP5 software developed at Los Alamos National Laboratory and distributed through RSICC (in the US) and the NEA Databank.
